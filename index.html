[ 06 — FAQ ]

Five things
people ask.

If yours isn't here, ask on the call. The intake is the FAQ.

What's an AI agent? +
A program that runs on your machine, takes instructions in plain English over a chat app, and does work — sends emails, queries your data, posts updates, books rooms. Like ChatGPT, except it can act on its own and live where you live.
Why not just use ChatGPT? +
ChatGPT answers in a tab. An agent works in the background. It has your tools, your data, your channels. No copy-paste. No leaving your messaging app. And nothing about it is rented from someone else's roadmap.
Is this secure? +
Local-first by default. Keys stay on your machine. If you go cloud-routed, traffic flows through OpenRouter — never through us. After handoff we have no access. You can rotate every credential the same day.
What hardware do I need? +
For local LLM: a Mac with 32GB+ unified memory, or a Linux box with a 12GB+ GPU. For cloud-routed: anything that runs Node. If you're not sure, send specs on the intake call — we'll tell you straight.
What if it breaks? +
Two weeks of tweaks are included on every tier. After that, message us — usually a 5-minute fix, no charge. Beyond that, the Operator tier exists for clients who want us on call.
