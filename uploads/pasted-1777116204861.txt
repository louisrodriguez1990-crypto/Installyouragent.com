Build a single-page landing site for "Install Your Agent" — a service that installs personal AI agents (OpenClaw, local LLMs, custom swarms) for clients who want the capability without the technical setup.

BRAND
- Name: Install Your Agent
- Domain: installyouragent.com
- Vibe: Operator energy. Technical but not nerdy. Confident, fast, premium-but-accessible. Think: a high-end installer, not a SaaS startup. Less "AI revolution," more "I show up, it works, you're done."
- Aesthetic: Dark mode default. Mono or near-mono palette with one sharp accent (electric green, cyan, or amber — pick what feels most "terminal/operator"). Sharp edges, generous whitespace, large confident type. No gradients, no glassmorphism, no stock AI brain illustrations.

STRUCTURE (single scroll, no nav)

1. HERO
   - Headline: "I install your AI agent. You run a team from your phone."
   - Subhead: One line — "OpenClaw, local LLMs, custom swarms. Done in a weekend. Yours forever."
   - Primary CTA button: "Book a 15-min intake" (Calendly placeholder link)
   - Secondary: "Watch the 3-min demo" (Loom placeholder)
   - Small trust line below: "Flat fee. No subscriptions. No vendor lock-in."

2. THE PROBLEM (one tight section)
   - Three short lines, large type:
     "AI agents can run your business."
     "Setting one up takes a weekend you don't have."
     "I do it for you."

3. WHAT YOU GET
   - 4-6 items, icon + one-line each:
     • OpenClaw installed on your machine or VPS
     • Connected to Telegram, WhatsApp, or your channel of choice
     • Local LLM configured (no more API bills) OR cloud-routed via OpenRouter
     • 5-10 custom skills tuned to your business
     • 30-min walkthrough Loom
     • 2 weeks of tweaks included

4. PRICING
   - 3 tiers, simple cards:
     • Starter — $1,500 — single machine, standard skills, Telegram
     • Pro — $3,000 — custom skills, local LLM, multi-channel
     • Operator — $5,000+ — multi-device, custom swarm, ongoing retainer
   - Each card has a "Book this" button

5. WHY ME (short, no fluff)
   - 3 lines max. Something like:
     "I've installed [N] agents. I use mine daily. I'll send you mine on the call."

6. FAQ (5 questions, accordion)
   - What's an AI agent? / Why not just use ChatGPT? / Is this secure? / What hardware do I need? / What if it breaks?

7. FOOTER CTA
   - Big, simple: "Ready? Book the call."
   - Calendly button
   - Email link

TECHNICAL
- Single HTML file, inline Tailwind (CDN ok)
- Mobile-first, looks sharp on iPhone
- One Loom embed placeholder, one Calendly embed placeholder, one Stripe payment link placeholder
- No animations beyond subtle hover states
- Total page should feel like it loads instantly and reads in 30 seconds

TONE RULES
- No "revolutionize," "unlock," "harness," "empower," or any AI-marketing cliché
- No emojis except maybe one in the hero
- Sentences short. Verbs strong. Confidence without hype.
- Read it out loud — if it sounds like a SaaS landing page, rewrite it.

Output: one .html file, ready to deploy.
